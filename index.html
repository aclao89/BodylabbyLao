About Me

I am trainer currently residing in Fremont, CA. I worked with a wide range of clients from athletes, to teenagers, to weekend warriors, and to everyday individuals who want to feel better, be stronger, healthier, and more confident.

No fluff or extra business marketing ploys here. Body Lab by Lao is a fitness and lifestyle brand, committed to cultivate a community of growth and well-being. Our bodies are very different so training programs are catered to my client's individual needs. No matter the case, I have evidence-based methods and principles to deliver results.

My ideal client is anyone with a goal, a positive attitude, and willingness to learn and work hard.
